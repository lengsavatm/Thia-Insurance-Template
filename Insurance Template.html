Click the button below to access your prescriptions discounts

•  To submit your prescription claims, you can either mail, fax, or upload the Itemized Bill to your member portal. You need to ask the pharmacist each time for an itemized bill. An itemized bill is NOT the same as a customer receipt. An itemized bill includes the type of prescription, the charge/amount of medications that you paid, and the name/address/phone number for the pharmacy. In addition, you will need to include your full name, member ID number, date of birth, address, and phone number. Please allow 6-10 weeks for processing.
